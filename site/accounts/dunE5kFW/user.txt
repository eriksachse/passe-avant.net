Bio:

Mine Kaplangı is the Mitgründerin des Kurator*innen Kollektivs çukurcuma in Istanbul, Türkei. Seit 2014 arbeitet sie als Kuratorin at BLOK Art Space, Istanbul. 2018 war sie Residenz Kuratorin bei Basis Frankfurt.

Mine Kaplangı is the co-founder of collective çukurcuma curatorial collective from Istanbul, Turkey. Since 2014 she has been working as an artist representative and curator at BLOK Art Space , Istanbul. She was curator in residence 2018 at Basis Frankfurt.
